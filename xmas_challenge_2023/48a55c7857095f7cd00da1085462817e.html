Puzzle 04 - How many presents?

In this challenge you need to calculate the total number of presents.

One possible solution could involve storing the number of presents from each day in a list and then adding them up at the end...

On the 1st day of Christmas my true love gave to me ... one present
On the 2nd day of Christmas my true love gave to me ... three presents
On the 3rd day of Christmas my true love gave to me ... the number of presents on the 2nd day plus four times the number of presents on the 1st day
On the 4th day of Christmas my true love gave to me ... the number of presents on the 3rd day plus four times the number of presents on the 2nd day
...
On the 12th day of Christmas my true love gave to me ... the number of presents on the 11th day plus four times the number of presents on the 10th day

How many presents did the lucky person get?
